---
title: "Word counter"
description: "Count text statistics locally without sending content to an LLM."
---

```bash
arka word-counter --file README.md --json
arka word-counter --text "Paste text here"
cat draft.md | arka word-counter --stdin --json
```

The skill reports words, unique words, characters, characters without spaces,
lines, sentences, and an estimated reading time at 200 words per minute. It
counts sentence boundaries first and includes a transparent rough estimate of
15 words per sentence (`sentence_word_estimate`) before reporting the exact
word count; this is useful for quickly checking whether a draft is in the
expected range.
